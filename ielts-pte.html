Overview of IELTS

The IELTS (International English Language Testing System) is a standardized English language test for non-native speakers. It is used for study, work, and migration purposes in countries like the UK, Canada, Australia, and more. IELTS assesses your skills in listening, reading, writing, and speaking through a structured test format. There are two versions: Academic, for higher education or professional registration, and General Training, for work or immigration. Scores are given on a band scale from 0 to 9, with most institutions requiring a band of 6.0 or higher.
