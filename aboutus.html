About Rinova International

Since 2010, Rinova International Educational Institute has been dedicated to empowering students in Nepal with the skills and opportunities needed to succeed globally. Our mission is to provide exceptional educational services tailored to each student's unique aspirations.

Contact Us

Our Mission and Vision

At Rinova, our mission is to empower students through education, fostering academic excellence and global opportunities. We envision a world where every student has access to personalized guidance and resources to achieve their full potential.

Empowerment: Equipping students with the tools for success.

Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in education.

Inclusivity: Creating opportunities for all students.

Our History

Founded in 2010, Rinova International Educational Institute began with a vision to bridge the gap between Nepalese students and global education. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted institution, helping thousands of students achieve their dreams through study abroad programs, language training, and personalized academic support.

2010: Established in Kathmandu, Nepal.

2015: Expanded programs to include computer and language classes.

2020: Launched online tutoring and virtual counseling services.
